# StopNCD.org

The Emory Global Diabetes Research Center, the Institute for People and Technology at Georgia Tech, and the Shankar Center of Excellence for Diabetes Research at IIT Madras are working with our partners to identify and solve the highest priority problems in noncommunicable diseases. By leveraging our combined expertise in medical sciences, engineering, and public health, we strive to bridge the gap between problems, research, and real-world translation of solutions, ensuring that the most innovative solutions reach the communities that need them.
