# Top 10 Problems

By identifying clear and actionable problems in noncommunicable diseases identified by patients and their caregivers, clinicians, and clinical staff, we aim to focus the innovations of researchers and technologists to have immediate real-world impact. Our collective experience in research and product development lead us to believe that the solutions developed through this approach will translate directly towards meeting the needs of people living with and treating noncommunicable diseases.
