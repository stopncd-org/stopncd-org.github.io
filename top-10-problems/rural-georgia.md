    <span class="prob-card-btn">More info</span>
  </a>

  </a>

    <span class="prob-card-btn">More info</span>
  </a>
  <a href="/top-10-problems/rural-georgia/problem6/" class="prob-card" style="background:#722985">
    <p class="prob-card-title">Medication Discontinuation from Side-Effects</p>
    <p class="prob-card-desc">Poor side-effect management and limited follow-up often drive patients to stop diabetes medications.</p>
    <span class="prob-card-btn">More info</span>
  </a>
  <a href="/top-10-problems/rural-georgia/problem7/" class="prob-card" style="background:#a31c44">
    <p class="prob-card-title">Limited Exercise Options</p>
    <p class="prob-card-desc">Rural communities lack safe, affordable spaces for exercise.</p>
    <span class="prob-card-btn">More info</span>
  </a>
  <a href="/top-10-problems/rural-georgia/problem8/" class="prob-card" style="background:#4ca146">
    <p class="prob-card-title">Diabetes Stigma and Awareness</p>
    <p class="prob-card-desc">Diabetes stigma and low community awareness fuel stress, isolation, and poor disease management.</p>
    <span class="prob-card-btn">More info</span>
  </a>
