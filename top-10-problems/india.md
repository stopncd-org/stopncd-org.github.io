    <p class="prob-card-title">Gaps in Provider Knowledge</p>
    <p class="prob-card-desc">Peripheral healthcare providers lack up-to-date knowledge of evidence-based diabetes prevention, screening, and management.</p>
    <span class="prob-card-btn">More info</span>
  </a>
  <a href="/top-10-problems/india/problem2/" class="prob-card" style="background:#1e97d4">
    <p class="prob-card-title">Limited Access to HbA1c Testing</p>
    <p class="prob-card-desc">Many patients lack access to routine testing for HbA1c, a more reliable marker of diabetes control and risk of complications.</p>
    <span class="prob-card-btn">More info</span>
  </a>
  <a href="/top-10-problems/india/problem3/" class="prob-card" style="background:#6766cc">
    <p class="prob-card-title">Inaccessible Diabetes Education</p>
    <p class="prob-card-desc">Millions of people struggle to access culturally relevant education in their language of preference for diabetes prevention and self-management.</p>
    <span class="prob-card-btn">More info</span>
  </a>
  <a href="/top-10-problems/india/problem4/" class="prob-card" style="background:#14496b">
    <p class="prob-card-title">Fragmented Patient Care</p>
    <p class="prob-card-desc">Poor referral and follow-up systems result in missed opportunities for timely diabetes care and intervention.</p>
    <span class="prob-card-btn">More info</span>
  </a>
  <a href="/top-10-problems/india/problem5/" class="prob-card" style="background:#648d1c">
    <p class="prob-card-title">Inconvenient Glucose Monitoring</p>
    <p class="prob-card-desc">Existing tools are invasive, intermittent, and expensive for long-term monitoring of blood glucose.</p>
    <span class="prob-card-btn">More info</span>
  </a>
  <a href="/top-10-problems/india/problem6/" class="prob-card" style="background:#722985">
    <p class="prob-card-title">Patient Burden for Insulin Delivery</p>
    <p class="prob-card-desc">Automated insulin delivery systems remain too expensive and inaccessible for most patients with type 1 diabetes who need them.</p>
    <span class="prob-card-btn">More info</span>
  </a>
  <a href="/top-10-problems/india/problem7/" class="prob-card" style="background:#a31c44">
    <p class="prob-card-title">Delayed Detection of Asymptomatic Foot Disease</p>
    <p class="prob-card-desc">More than half of diabetic foot disease cases remain asymptomatic until late stages, highlighting the need for early detection.</p>
    <span class="prob-card-btn">More info</span>
  </a>
  <a href="/top-10-problems/india/problem8/" class="prob-card" style="background:#4ca146">
    <p class="prob-card-title">Inappropriate Footwear</p>
    <p class="prob-card-desc">Patients lack affordable, protective footwear that can prevent foot ulcers and amputations.</p>
    <span class="prob-card-btn">More info</span>
  </a>
  <a href="/top-10-problems/india/problem9/" class="prob-card" style="background:#da5f16">
    <p class="prob-card-title">Inadequate Access to Retinopathy Screening</p>
    <p class="prob-card-desc">Retinal damage from diabetes often goes undetected due to the lack of affordable and accessible eye screening solutions.</p>
    <span class="prob-card-btn">More info</span>
  </a>
  <a href="/top-10-problems/india/problem10/" class="prob-card" style="background:#007cbd">
    <p class="prob-card-title">Singular Focus on Glucose</p>
    <p class="prob-card-desc">Physicians rely predominantly on blood glucose and lack access to behavioural, physiological, and environmental indicators important for diabetes management.</p>
    <span class="prob-card-btn">More info</span>
  </a>
