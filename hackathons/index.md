# Why Are We Organizing Innovation Competitions?

We are providing a platform where creative minds can develop unique solutions for key problems in noncommunicable diseases. Our experience shows that bringing together teams of enthusiastic and driven students is an effective step towards building a pipeline of engineers and innovators who will develop a wide array of potential solutions.
