## What is stopncd.org?

Noncommunicable diseases, such as diabetes, cardiovascular conditions and cancer, kill more than 41 million people globally each year. Our collaboration aims to provide a platform to not only raise awareness of key problems faced by people living with and treating noncommunicable diseases and more importantly, facilitate the development of solutions to those problems that can be solved using technology.

We are identifying [Top 10 Problems](/top-10-problems/) across different NCDs faced by patients, their caregivers, and clinicians that can be solved using technology. [We encourage entrepreneurs and innovators to work on solutions to these problems!](/hackathons/)
